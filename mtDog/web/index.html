Your sensitive genomic data is yours
mtDog allows your to run an analysis of your genomic data locally on your own device. No one else can access these results, not even us.
Share your results without sharing your personal data
After running an analysis of your genetic data, mtDog will add an attestation of your results to your DNA Passport. Providers can consult and verify your results with these attestations rather having to handle any of the sensitive data themselves.
